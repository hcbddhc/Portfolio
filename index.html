Visual Definition

Font

The font used will be Quicksand for its great readibility. Additionally, it gives off this really casual and comforting feeling which better reflects my personality and design philosophy.

Color

I attempted to go for a "dark mode" color palette, not only to experiment because I've been mainly using white for most of my past projects, I also belive a darker color scheme better represents me. As dark mode emphasizes the importance of user experience, used to prevent eye strains and such. My designs have the same emphasis. I also picked to go for a slight dark blue to reinforce this feeling of calmness.
